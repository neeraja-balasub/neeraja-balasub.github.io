Hi! I am a PhD Candidate in Microbial Genomics (within the Environmental Sciences Graduate Program) at The Ohio State University, advised by Prof. Karen Dannemiller. I am also an NGS & Bioinformatics Research Intern, within the NGS & Special Projects Team at Abbott Nutrition.

I am looking for industry, academic & postdoc roles starting April 2026.

Research

  • My research focuses on investigating environmental microbiomes and food pathogens using microbial genomics and bioinformatics. My skillset spans RNAseq, 16s/ITS rRNA, metagenomics and Whole Genome Sequencing (WGS) bioinformatics, in addition to molecular biology lab techniques.

  • I also work on developing scalable and reproducible bioinformatics using Nextflow and Containers on cloud and HPC platforms.
